How do I analyze the news?

Today, there is a wealth of available and accessible information. Do you want to learn how to harness this information and create stories and visualizations? If you are a beginner, this may seem challenging. This site guides you from downloading APIs, data cleaning, creating visualizations, and finally how to host your work on the web. Our examples center on the NPR API, though there are many open data sources out there!
